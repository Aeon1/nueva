Gastronomy

Ther is a lot of typical saucers

It is plenty of traditional dishes: machaca, caldillo, picadillo, rice, pineapple tamales,meat, corn and sweet tamales; guacabaqui and barbacoa. In El Fuerte Magic Town it has become a tradition the typical dishes made of Black Bass: fish chicharrones, scallops, fishball soup, fish sarandeado style, seafood mixed cocktails and freshwater lobster.

As a dessert you can find: pepitorias, jamoncillos, cocadas, capirotada, tacuarines (coricos), coyotas, semitas, melcocha, buñuelos, gorditas de queso.

Fun activities

Fun activities

Walk by the River

The Constitución Street, walking towards the west, take us directly to wide staircase that take us directly to the Malecon beside the Río Fuerte. Place where you can enjoy an early morning run or admire the nature.

Take a Tour to La Galera

Site of familiar recreation on the beside the Fuerte River, there are seafood Restaurants, walking trail, shade trees, wide traffic parking lot and a suspension bridge to cross the broad river admiring the flow of the calm waters and, if it is interested to know the history of this region, it will be able to continue the way towards the Cerro de la Máscara: archaeological site that encloses the major number of petroglyphs of Sinaloa.

Raft Rides

Ride on the Fuerte River, watch the birds and enjoy pure nature. It is the source of the life and of the development of all the Sinaloa's north. It is born in Chihuahua and does a tour of more than six hundred kilometres feeding three dams that control his waters to irrigate thousands of hectares creating the richest agricultural zone of the field sinaloense. There are two dams that are a very important attraction: Miguel Hidalgo and the Josefa Ortiz de Domínguez.

Historical places

The historical center of El Fuerte is a beautiful sample of the colonial architecture and of the 19th century. The buildings preserve the neoclassic style. Where it is possible to observe his different attractions as El Fuerte's Foundation Mural at the City Hall, Plaza de Armas, The Church of Jesus' Sacred Heart, Los Portales, The House of The Culture or House of The Vault, The Constitution's House, El Fuerte Museum and Observation Point, The Orrantia Mansion and the traditional Alleys.
